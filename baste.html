05/13/2021

Please be advised that Today, May 13, 2021, has been declared a regular holiday by President Rodrigo Duterte in observance of Eid'l Fitr (Feast of Ramadan). Work operations in the School are therefore suspended today, and will resume on May 14, 2021 (Friday). SSC-R de Cavite joins the whole nation in extending warm wishes of a happy and peaceful celebration of Ramadan to our Muslim brothers and sisters.
